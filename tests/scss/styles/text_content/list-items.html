List Items

StageHands will apply the following styles to all LI elements.

font-family: globals.$neutralFontFamily;
line-height: globals.$neutralTextLine;
font-size: globals.$neutralTextHeight;

If the LI directly follows another LI StageHands will apply the following styles;

margin: globals.$margin;

If the LI directly follows another OL OR UL StageHands will apply the following styles;

margin: globals.$margin;
