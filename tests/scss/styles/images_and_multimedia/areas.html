Areas

StageHands will apply the following styles to all AREA elements.

StageHands currently does not apply any styles to this element by default.

Please note that by default Mapped Areas do not translate well with fluid images. That is the position of the areas are fixed units and will not conform to the size of the fluid image.

Mapped areas will not overflow out of the boundary of the image,so you don't need to worry about the areas covering other pieces of content.( An example of this is the Red bar in the image bellow. The areas coordinates for y2 extend beyond the bounds of the image. Yet no clickable sections are present below the image.)
